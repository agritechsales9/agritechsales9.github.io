AGRI_TECH FARMING CALENDER

Tropical Climates

PeriodActivity
Jan - MarLand preparation and nursery establishment
Apr - JunMain planting season (maize, beans, bananas)
Jul - SepWeeding, pest control, mid-season harvesting
Oct - DecHarvesting and post-harvest handling

Arid Climates

PeriodActivity
Jan - MarSoil conservation, water harvesting structures
Apr - JunPlant drought-resistant crops (millet, sorghum)
Jul - SepMinimal irrigation, crop maintenance
Oct - DecHarvest, storage, livestock care

Temperate Climates

PeriodActivity
Jan - MarGreenhouse or indoor seed starting
Apr - JunField planting and crop management
Jul - SepFull harvest of fruits and vegetables
Oct - DecSoil amendment, winter crops

Cold Climates

PeriodActivity
Jan - MarPlanning, tool maintenance, indoor sprouting
Apr - JunShort-season planting, frost monitoring
Jul - SepHarvest root crops, hardy vegetables
Oct - DecCover cropping, cold-frame gardening

General Tips for All Farmers

  • Monitor weather forecasts and adapt
  • Use compost and organic matter to enrich soil
  • Practice crop rotation and intercropping
  • Harvest water and irrigate efficiently
  • Ensure proper crop storage and pest control
